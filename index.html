Update v0.1.3a

Added 1 new card:

  • Tesla Coil, which does 50 damage and then 1/7 of the damage to all other cards. It also applies the Shock effect to all cards, which multiplies their health by 0.8, but multiplies their attack by 1.2

Other Additions:

  • Added some more card art
  • Introduction of Action cards (changed cards like Armageddon to action cards)
  • Bandit now steals instead of generates mana.
  • Custom Mode: Adds fun modifiers like Industrial Revolution (only get factories) or decay, where all of your codes slowly lose health.

Balance Changes:

  • Armageddon now costs mana
  • Bandit now has a 1/2 chance of gaining mana and steals 20-80% of the opponent's mana.

Bug Fixes:

  • Transportation Bus has been fixed
  • Armageddon will update after use
  • Supply Crate will now have a minimum of 2 ammo gain.
