Update v0.2.1a - QoL Changes! (part 2)

CARD ABILITY STATS!

Cards like the weakener and soul keeper now have ability stats. This means that their abilities will have an upgradable stat. For example, the weakener's base ability stat is 10, which means they gain 10 atk when attacking and take away 20 atk from the opponent. The stat increase is 3, so when you upgrade, it'll take away 26 atk and gain 13 atk.

Other Additions:

  • Relics, which are upgrades that can give you abilities like +10% health and other things.
  • You can now skip dialogue!
  • In stores, hovering over a relic will show its description, so you don't buy random stuff for no reason.

Balance Changes:

  • Janjo can now blast your cards with flames, doing 15 damage to each!
  • Turret now gains more atk, but loses 2/3 of it when reloading.

Bug Fixes:

  • You can now buy a relic once a reroll. Previously, you could spam the relic buy button and get overpowered relics.
